About Festival

Welcome to the future of tech at DEXT FORCE Festival! 

Immerse yourself in a groundbreaking celebration of innovation and decentralization. Join us for an electrifying experience where cutting-edge technologies, inspiring speakers, and a vibrant community converge with great vibes, non-stop music and fun activities.

All in a monumental location: La Llotja de Mar, a very large and spectacular historical luxury space in the heart of Barcelona. Don’t miss out on the next DEXT FORCE Festival!
